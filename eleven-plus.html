Eleven plus tuition with Right Way Learning

Give your child the best opportunity to earn a place at a high performing grammar school.

We offer your child access to experienced tutors while in their final years of primary school. The Eleven Plus acts as an entrance exam for grammar schools which can offer a number of benefits to a comprehensive school. These selective schools can offer you and your child another option if the state schools in your area are not up to standard.

3 stages for 11+ success

Right Way Learning provides students in the Birmingham area with bespoke tailored tuition plans, taught in a dedicated classroom environment to support and encourage their overall growth. We ensure success by:

  • Tutoring in relevant maths and English skills for the 11 Plus grammar school exams. Content and skill building across NumericaI, Verbal and Non-Verbal Reasoning
  • Pupils undertake bespoke mock exams under strict time restrictions and our qualified teachers use these exams to further strengthen understanding.
  • 10 minute speed tests – combining robust accuracy with speed to prepare pupils for the high-stress exam environment

From the outset, it is important to view the 11+ as a process of real value which will ultimately improve core skills in Maths and English, whilst providing a great foundation for secondary education.
